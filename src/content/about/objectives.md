## To build a standardized, universal guide

We aim to establish a common framework for anuran acoustic monitoring that can be applied across research groups and geographic regions. Through a standardized protocol for data acquisition, recording, annotation and analysis, we seek to ensure that datasets are consistent, interoperable, and reusable, creating a shared and practical language among researchers worldwide.

## To create an open access platform and network

We are committed to making anuran acoustic data easily discoverable and freely accessible. By using and developing open-source AI tools and promoting the exchange of code and models, we aim to foster collaboration across research projects and reduce barriers within the scientific community.
