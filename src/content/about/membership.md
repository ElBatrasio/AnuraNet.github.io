# Become a member

At the AnuraNet network we are focused on accelerating the use of AI for anuran acoustic monitoring and research.
We invite participation from researchers, conservation practitioners, developers, and students with an active interest in this field.
We welcome individuals from all backgrounds who wish to contribute to the collaborative development of AI tools for anuran conservation and research.

To apply for membership, please complete the form linked below.
